About IPEC Ghaziabad...

Inderprastha Engineering College Ghaziabad is an affiliated college established in 2000. IPEC Ghaziabad offers UG and PG courses to students in the disciplines of engineering. B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech courses are offered to students at IPEC. Admissions at IPEC Ghaziabad are on the basis of merit in entrance exams. Prospective students must appear for entrance exams such as, JEE Main , UPCET and GATE as per the eligibility requirements.

IPEC Ghaziabad is affiliated to Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. They offer scholarships to students as a reward for their academic excellence. The institute has implemented a policy to reward university toppers, providing full exemption fromtuition fees to students ranking in the top 10 positions at the university level.

Training and Placement at IPEC Ghaziabad is responsible for bringing top-tier companies to the campus. Some of the leading companies include Airtel, TCS, Wipro, and others. Amenities such as a library, cafeteria, medical facilities, auditorium, laboratories, comfortable accommodation and many others for a comfortable learning experience.
